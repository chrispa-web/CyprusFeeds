Κύπρος Live

Open civic data for Cyprus residents

What is this?

Κύπρος Live is a free, open-source civic dashboard showing key economic indicators for Cyprus in real time. No ads. No tracking. No accounts.

Data Sources

  • ⛽ EC Oil Bulletin — weekly fuel prices
  • ⚡ Eurostat nrg_pc_204 — electricity prices
  • 📈 Eurostat HICP — inflation
  • 👥 Eurostat LFS — unemployment
  • 💧 Cyprus WDD — water dam levels (coming soon)
  • 🚧 Cyprus Police News — road works & closures

How to install

iPhone / iPad: Tap the Share button in Safari, then "Add to Home Screen".

Android: Tap the menu (⋮) in Chrome, then "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App".

Data refreshes automatically · Cached for offline use
Open source — not affiliated with the Republic of Cyprus
